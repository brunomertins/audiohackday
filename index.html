AUDIO HACK DAY

@Campus Party EU

Campus Party Europe is taking over Berlin Tempelhof for six days of tech, hacking and well-supervised craziness. SoundCloud is excited to be joining Campus Party Europe on August 23rd for a special Audio Hack Day hackathon.

Audio Hack Day is a 24 hour hack event where people of all interests - developers, designers, or anyone with a good idea - get together to hack on the most innovative, coolest or quirkiest audio app possible.
