AUDIO HACK DAY

@Campus Party EU

Campus Party Europe is taking over Berlin Tempelhof for six days of tech, hacking and well-supervised craziness. SoundCloud is excited to be joining Campus Party Europe on August 23rd for a special Audio Hack Day hackathon.

AHD is a 24-hour hack event where people of all different backgrounds, be it developer, designer or just someone with a good idea, come together to hack together the coolest, most innovative, funniest, or strangest audio app possible.
